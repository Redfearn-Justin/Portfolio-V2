About Me

My name is Justin Redfearn, and I am a Full Stack Web Developer! Starting in early 2018, I began my journey into the wonderful realm of Web Development, with no prior knowledge of anything coding-related. After a grooling six month bootcamp, I emerged victorious, truimphant, and a yearning for more. Now, I begin another journey: to use this newfound knowledge, interest, and passion, and continue to seek more of its complex and intricate splendor.

When I'm not coding a mile a minute, I am exploring my other hobbies. Whether it be running, playing my guitar or electric bass, learning a new language, playing a video game, or reading the latest Spiderman comic, I tend to indulge in the things that stimulate my mind and encourage creativity - in whatever form it may come to me.
